Directions

From Campus Entrance 1
To Teva
Steps
  1. Exit the security booth at Entrance 1 and immediately go up the first flight of stairs.
  2. Continue straight for about 250 meters until you reach another staircase on the left, with a path descending toward the main road on the right. Take the staircase going up.
  3. Continue for another 250 meters until you reach a bridge and cross it. After crossing the bridge, veer left into the plaza area.
  4. At the entrance to the plaza, turn left and walk alongside the building until you reach a staircase going down.
  5. Go down the staircase and continue along the path, passing the International School on your left.
  6. At the next intersection, veer right and stay on the path until you reach the building.
